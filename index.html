Teaching Philosophy

We at BitsMaximus believe that teaching is an active exercise, where each student presents a unique challenge. Every individual has a distinct way of understanding concepts and a unique set of prior knowledge. To ensure personalized attention and interactive learning, our online classes are limited to six students. This small group setting encourages open discussion, peer learning, and allows the instructor to adapt to each student's learning pace.

Why is the class size limited to 6 students?

Every individual has a distinct way of understanding concepts. With 6 students, you can ask questions without hesitation, interact with fellow students, and get individual attention. The instructor can evaluate progress during class and adjust the pace accordingly. It's more affordable than 1-on-1 coaching while avoiding the drawbacks of overcrowded classrooms.
