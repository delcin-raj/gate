Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the class size limited to 6 students?

Every individual has a distinct way of understanding concepts. With 6 students, you can ask questions without hesitation, interact with fellow students, and get individual attention. The instructor can evaluate progress during class and adjust the pace accordingly. It's more affordable than 1-on-1 coaching while avoiding the drawbacks of overcrowded classrooms.
